Sometimes it can be easier to display a small window of the data, and allow the user to pan the window through the data. Axes can be made to allow panning and zooming with chart.setInteractiveAxis(interactiveAxis).

Use the mousewheel to zoom in/out of the chart, and click-drag to pan through the data.

var xAxis = new insight.Axis('AppCount', insight.scales.linear); chart.setInteractiveAxis(xAxis);
